Eclipse projects. Here's how the projects were restructured:

* res/ => openjava/src/main/res/
* src/ => openjava/src/main/java/
* src/org/opencv/engine/OpenCVEngineInterface.aidl => openjava/src/main/aidl/org/opencv/engine/OpenCVEngineInterface.aidl
